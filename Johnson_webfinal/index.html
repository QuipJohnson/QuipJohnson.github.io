Hey! I’m Quip Johnson, an illustration/journalism double major at City College of San Francisco and an aspiring freelancer and sequential artist. I took Visual Media Design 105 as part of the requirements for my journalism certificate, but also because it seemed like a good way to get a handle on a couple Adobe products I’d never worked with before. Aesthetically, I love working with clean, modern fonts, and I’m a huge fan of complementary colors like orange and teal!

Reptile Magazine || Adobe Photoshop

I’ve worked with Photoshop since I first used Elements in 2012. That being said, I’m used to using it for digital painting, not photo manipulation, so this was a new experience for me. I based my initial design on a minimalistic magazine about reptiles. I knew the project instructions said to add a selfie, but when I realized the only pictures of me were all in odd positions or at weird angles, I had to get creative. I used a photo of a reptile tank and added myself in the various nooks and crannies, painting over myself at low opacity to adjust lighting manually.

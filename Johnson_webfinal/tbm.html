Egg || Adobe Animate

I’ve never worked with Animate either, and though it’s never been on the top of my list of programs to learn, I’m really glad I got a chance to try it out. It wasn’t nearly as time-consuming or difficult as I thought, and this project turned out to be a ton of fun. This is my animation, which was based off of Guillermo del Toro’s Oscar-award winning film The Shape of Water. The movie features an amphibian man who the main character feeds hard-boiled eggs to. This mini-animation was about the process of waking up and boiling eggs, with a similar color scheme and, hopefully, ambiance to that of the source material.

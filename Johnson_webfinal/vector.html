Waxworm Nutrition || Adobe Illustrator

Illustrator was the program I was most excited to experiment with during this class. I’d never used it, but I was fully aware of how useful vector-based art could be. I really enjoyed working with it, and I now feel confident enough that I will be able to find my way around Illustrator in the future. I based this project off of a sketch I’d done of an amphibian, a frog specifically. The instructions made it clear that both text and a food source were necessary, so I added waxworms, a frog delicacy, and made up a little table of nutrition facts and information so he could be certain he was getting his calcium for the day.
